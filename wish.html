Wish

Wish is the idea of a platform meant for interactive stories. I've written and visualized an example story through the use of animating SVG with Javascript. That story is told in an interactive way to enhance the experience in reading it.

Click here to experience it yourself! But note: it's optimized for the iPad. Please change your viewport if you're on desktop/mobile.
