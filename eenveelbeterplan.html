Assignment

"Een veel beter plan" (or "A much better plan") is a story conceived, written and hosted on several platforms (a website and via epub) that's to do with climate change and, more specifically, climate change denial. It's a narrative that can end in three different ways, based on a quiz you, as the reader, can take. The assignment was about creating a cross-media story based on a plot that can affect and speak to everyone. So I thought in terms of giant problems that affect everyone's health, and everybody's future health as well: climate change. A big component to the assignment was to build a community around the story. So, I had come up with a debate community, where one could challenge or nominate prominent and active users/readers of the story. It's all conceptual, it's not realised into its technical possibilities as that was not the point, so you'll find pictures and descriptions of this "community" instead.
